“Hey fellas, guess what I heard today,” Philip cold-opened to the crew.

“What Philip,” his cousin Jeffrey chirped irritatedly.

“Our hearts beat over 1,000 times a minute!” Philip squawked, barely able to contain himself.

“That’s great Philip, now fuck off,” his brother-in-law, Keith, said to him. Then they all turned back to their game of hummingbird poker.

Philip wasn’t surprised. They cared not for the intricacies of existence, but Philip was different. He buzzed off to think a bit.
